# BidSpirit - Judaica Specialization

## Overview
BidSpirit is a specialized auction platform focused exclusively on **Judaica** - Jewish ceremonial objects, religious artifacts, and cultural items.

## Our Specialty
We specialize in authenticating, cataloging, and auctioning:

- **Menorahs** - Hanukkah menorahs (chanukiah) from various periods and regions
- **Torah Scrolls** - Complete and partial scrolls with provenance
- **Ceremonial Silver** - Kiddush cups, Havdalah sets, Seder plates
- **Religious Textiles** - Tallitot, Parochet, Torah covers
- **Holiday Items** - Dreidels, Megillot Esther, Shofars
- **Antique Judaica** - Pre-war European pieces, American Judaica, Israeli artifacts
- **Books & Manuscripts** - Rare Hebrew texts, prayer books, historical documents

## Target Audience
- Collectors of Judaica
- Synagogues and religious institutions
- Museums and cultural centers
- Families seeking heirlooms
- Scholars and researchers

## Authentication & Provenance
All items undergo expert authentication and include detailed provenance documentation to ensure authenticity and historical significance.
